More Than Cricket.
A Vision For The Future.

Melbourne Super League was established in 2021 with a bold vision — to create a platform where cricket becomes a bridge between cultures, communities and opportunity.

What began as a community-driven initiative has rapidly evolved into one of Australia's fastest-growing multicultural sporting organisations, delivering professionally managed competitions, elite player pathways and meaningful community impact.

At MSL, cricket is more than a game. It is a vehicle for inclusion, leadership and inspiration. Every tournament we deliver is designed to create opportunities for talented players, strengthen local communities and celebrate the cultural diversity that makes Australian cricket unique.

Today, Melbourne Super League continues to raise the standard of multicultural cricket through innovation, professionalism and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Building A Legacy

2021

Foundation

Melbourne Super League was founded with a vision to unite communities through cricket while creating meaningful opportunities for talented players from multicultural backgrounds.

2021–2024

Growing Roots

MSL established itself as a respected community cricket organisation, building strong partnerships with clubs, councils and community groups while growing participation across Victoria.

Dec 2024

MSL Premiers Launches

A new chapter began with the launch of MSL Premiers, Australia's first premium multicultural franchise-based T20 competition. The inaugural season introduced a new standard of competition, combining elite cricket, professional event delivery and community celebration.

Dec 2025

Bigger Than Ever

Building on the success of the inaugural season, MSL Premiers returned bigger than ever with expanded participation, enhanced broadcasting, international players, increased prize money and greater national recognition. The league cemented its reputation as one of Australia's premier multicultural sporting events.

The Future

What's Next

Melbourne Super League is entering an exciting new era of growth. Our long-term vision includes national expansion, stronger community partnerships, enhanced player pathways, international collaborations and the continued development of Australia's leading multicultural cricket ecosystem. Our journey is only just beginning.

Governance

Professional Leadership.
Sustainable Growth.

Melbourne Super League is committed to the highest standards of governance, transparency and operational excellence. Our experienced leadership team works closely with councils, clubs, commercial partners and community organisations to deliver competitions that are professionally managed, financially sustainable and focused on long-term growth.

The Future

The future of Melbourne Super League extends beyond tournaments. Our ambition is to become Australia's most influential multicultural cricket organisation by expanding nationally, developing elite talent pathways, strengthening community programs and delivering internationally recognised sporting events.

Every season represents another step towards building a stronger future for cricket.
